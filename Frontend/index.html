Frequently Asked Questions

Browse doctors on the Doctors page or scroll down to the doctor slider on the home page. Click "Book Appointment" on any doctor card. If you're logged in, a booking modal will appear where you can select a date and time. If not logged in, you'll be prompted to log in first.

Click the "Account" button in the top navigation bar and select "Register". Fill in your name, age, gender, phone number, email, and password. Your account will be created instantly and you'll be logged in automatically.

Click "Account" in the navbar and select "Login". Enter your registered phone number and password. Upon successful login, a JWT token is securely stored and you'll have full access to your appointments and booking features.

Go to "My Appointments" from the navbar or your account dropdown. Find the appointment you wish to cancel and click the "Cancel" button. You'll receive a confirmation notification. Please cancel at least 2 hours before the appointment time.

If a time slot is already taken by another patient, you'll see an error notification. Simply choose a different date or time. Slots are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
