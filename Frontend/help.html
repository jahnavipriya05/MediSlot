Getting Started

How to Use MediSlot

Follow these simple steps to book your appointment.

📅 How to Book an Appointment

1

Register as a Patient

Go to the Register Patient tab. Fill in your name, age, gender, phone, and email. Submit the form.

2

Note Your Patient ID

After registration, your unique Patient ID appears on screen. Write it down — you'll need it every time you book.

3

Browse Doctors

Scroll to the Doctors section. Each card shows the doctor's name, specialization, and email.

4

Click "Book Appointment"

Tap the amber button on any doctor card. You'll be taken to the booking form with that doctor pre-selected.

5

Enter Your Details & Confirm

Enter your Patient ID, choose a date and time, and click "Book Appointment". A success message confirms your slot.

🔍 How to View Your Appointments

1

Go to "View Appointments" Tab

Click the "🔍 View Appointments" tab in the app section on the Home page.

2

Enter Your Patient ID

Type in the Patient ID you received when you registered, then click Search.

3

View All Appointments

All your appointments will appear as cards, showing the doctor, date, and time for each.

4

Cancel If Needed

Click the red "🗑 Cancel" button on any appointment card to remove it. A confirmation dialog will appear first.

Pro Tips

Get the Most Out of MediSlot

💡 Tip: Click "Book Appointment" directly on a doctor's card — it pre-fills the doctor in the booking form for you!
💡 Tip: You can't pick a date in the past — the date picker automatically restricts to today and future dates.
⚠️ Important: Always save your Patient ID after registering. There is currently no "forgot ID" recovery on the frontend.
✅ Good to know: Appointments are confirmed instantly. If the slot is already taken, you'll get a clear error message — no guessing!
