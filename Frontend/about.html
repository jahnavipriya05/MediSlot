About Us

What is MediSlot?

A modern appointment platform built to simplify healthcare for patients and doctors alike.

Our Mission

Bridging Patients & Doctors Seamlessly

MediSlot was built to solve a simple but painful problem — booking a doctor's appointment shouldn't feel like a bureaucratic maze. We created a lightweight, fast, and intuitive platform that connects patients with healthcare professionals in a matter of seconds.

Whether you're a patient looking for the right specialist or a clinic managing its doctor roster, MediSlot keeps everything organized and collision-free.

Our Purpose

Why We Built MediSlot

Healthcare access is a fundamental right. We believe technology can make it easier for everyone.

🕐

Save Time

Patients spend an average of 30+ minutes trying to book appointments over phone. MediSlot cuts that to under 2 minutes — from any device, any time.

🌐

Always Available

Our platform works 24/7. Book an appointment at midnight for tomorrow morning, without worrying about clinic working hours.

📊

Organized Records

Doctors and clinics can maintain clean records of all appointments, patient details, and schedules without spreadsheets or paper logs.

For Patients

Benefits For You

Everything designed with the patient experience in mind.

1

One-time Registration

Register once with your name, age, gender, phone, and email. Your Patient ID is stored and reused for every future booking.

2

Browse Qualified Doctors

View all registered doctors with their specializations. Tap "Book Appointment" on any doctor card to jump straight into scheduling.

3

Choose Your Slot

Pick a date and time. If a slot is already taken, MediSlot will notify you immediately — no wasted trips to the clinic.

4

View & Cancel Anytime

Look up all your appointments by Patient ID. Cancel any appointment with a single click if your plans change.

Under the Hood

How MediSlot is Built

⚙️
Backend — FastAPI + MySQL + SQLAlchemy

High-performance Python REST API with full CRUD endpoints.

🎨
Frontend — HTML + CSS + JavaScript

Pure vanilla frontend with no frameworks. Clean and beginner-friendly.

🔗
Integration — Fetch API

All data flows through RESTful HTTP calls to the backend running on localhost:8000.
